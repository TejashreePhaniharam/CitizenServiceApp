OUR NOTION
We aim to increase awareness in the public and encourage them to help those who are at a forfeit in physical or relational aspects and in need of proper shelter and care. Organizations which are not recognized properly and need social support in funding and equipement can make themselves public and available through this platform to public and get an aid. This platform provides place for both public to find organizations and organizations to find public for support and also manage their organizations online giving them flexible accessibility and avoiding the risk of losing hard copies and theft. This is a safe and secure platform available to everyone. Building a family called nation.
